Justin Vernon

Bon Iver performing in Stockholm, Sweden in 2011

Justin Vernon, the creative force behind the indie folk
Bon Iver, is a singer-songwriter and producer whose ethereal music has captivated audiences worldwide. Born and raised in
Eau Claire, Wisconsin, Vernon's musical journey began with the band DeYarmond Edison before he ventured into a solo project that would later become Bon Iver. His hauntingly beautiful vocals and introspective lyrics have defined the band's sound, earning critical acclaim and a dedicated fan base.


Beyond Bon Iver, Vernon's musical genius extends to collaborations with artists across genres, from Kanye West to The National. His deeply personal songwriting, often drawing from themes of nature and introspection, has resonated with listeners, making him a pivotal figure in the indie music scene. Justin Vernon continues to inspire with his soulful melodies, cementing his place as a visionary in modern music.
